SDLC and Flowcharting


I am sure no one used the SDLC method anymore after the assignment, even though it is a very important step in developing software.

Using SDLC and flowcharts, students map out how their program will function. Since they are only required to create animations in Processing, many feel there is no use for a flowchart as their program is very basic and only has one function and no inputs.

Personally, I did not use a flowchart for any of my Processing programs because they are very basic in function. However, if I were to be asked to create a more complicated program, such as one with inputs and decisions, a flowchart would definitely be helpful.
